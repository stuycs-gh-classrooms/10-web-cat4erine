About

This is their fifth studio album, released on September 9, 2013. It is the commercially the most successful album for the band and was the first to be influenced by hip hop.

It draws inspiration from a wide range of genres, including "psychedelic rock, blues rock, hard rock, heavy metal, desert rock, R&B, and soul." Its lyrics convey darker themes of "tainted romance and loneliness."
